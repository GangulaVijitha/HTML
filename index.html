Araku Valley Vizag

One of the oldest port cities in India, Visakhapatnam is also a major industrial center. It is gaining popularity as a significant tourism destination in India. Also known as Vizag, and ‘Goa of East Coast’, it offers ample opportunities for a day’s outing to escape the chaotic and busy life through a fun-filled coastal vacation

  3. Araku Coffee Plantations/Galikonda View Point
  4. Araku Tribal Museum
  5. Araku Coffee Museum
  6. Padmapuram Botanical Garden
